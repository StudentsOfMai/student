Kansas City Chiefs

This site is here to provide informtion about how the 3 previous seasons went for the KC Chiefs.

The Kansas City Chiefs are a team of the NFL (National Football League).

We have highlighted main events from 3 years that are:

  • 2016-17
  • 2017-18
  • 2018-19

We will now bring you back to 2016-17.

2016-17 from the Chief's perspective:

The Chief's 2016-17 year had a rank of 12 wins and 4 losses.

A game that particularly stands out is the game of week 8 against the Indianapolis Colts that was an away game for the Chiefs at Lucas Oil Stadium.

In this game the Chiefs won by 16 points with a score of 30-14 bringing their season over-all up to 5 win and 2 losses.

The Chief's bye week was week 5.

In the 2016-17 season the Chiefs had 7 pro-bowlers:

  • Safety-Eric Berry+Team MVP
  • Punt Receiver-Tyreek Hill
  • Quarterback-Alex Smith
  • Punter-Dustin Colquitt
  • Tight End-Travis Kelce
  • Cornerback-Marcus Peters
  • Special Teams-D.J. Alexander

2017-18 from the Chief's perspective

The Chiefs over-all was of 10 wins and 6 losses.

A game that particularly stands out is the game of week 1 against the New England Patriots.

This was an away game for the Chiefs at Gillette Stadium that Chiefs won by 15 points with a score of 42–27.

The Chief's bye week was week 10.

This year the Chiefs had 4 Pro-Bowlers:

  • Punt Receiver-Tyreek Hill
  • Quarterback-Alex Smith+Team MVP
  • Tight End-Travis Kelce
  • Running Back-Kareem Hunt

2018-19 from the Chief's perspective

The Chief's over-all was of 12 wins and 4 losses.

A game that particularly stands out is the game of week 7 against the Cincinnati Bengals.

This was a home game at Arrowhead Stadium and Chiefs won by 35 points with a score of 45-10.

This year the Chiefs had 6 Pro-Bowlers:

  • Quarterback-Patrick Mahomes+Team MVP
  • Linebacker-Dee Ford
  • Fullback-Anthony Sherman
  • Tight End-Travis Kelce
  • Wide-Receiver-Tyreek Hill
  • Tackle-Eric Fisher
